Two great ways to start collecting Nanrs and monetize your content

I have a blog or website

Add this script tag to the head or body of your blog or webiste.
{{scriptTag}}
Then add this tag wherever you want a Nanr button to appear. You can change the "nanr-size" attribute to make the button as large or small as you want.
{{generateButtonTag(tag.id)}}

I have a social media or other platform

Just direct people to this link and they will be taken to your Nanr stand where they can send you Nanrs
{{generateStandLink(tag.username)}}
